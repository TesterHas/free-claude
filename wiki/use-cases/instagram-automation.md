# Instagram & TikTok Automation

**Source:** Sabrina Ramonov — Instagram Stories (April 2026), Instagram Carousels (April 2026), TikTok Slideshows (May 2026)

## What Sabrina Covers

Three separate automation workflows for social media content:

1. **Instagram Stories** — automated creation via Make.com & n8n (13 min)
2. **Instagram Carousels** — automated creation in N8N and Make.com (18 min)
3. **TikTok Slideshows with Music** — automated creation via n8n and Make.com (14 min)

## Tools in the stack

- **n8n** — self-hosted automation builder
- **Make.com** (formerly Integromat) — cloud automation
- AI image/video generation for content

## The Pattern

All three follow the same pattern:
1. Input: topic, theme, or raw content
2. AI generates visual content (slides, images, video clips)
3. n8n/Make.com handles the posting pipeline
4. Output: published posts on schedule

No manual creation. The creator touches the strategy, not the execution.

## Selling This

This is the cleanest productization for social media managers and content agencies:
- Input: content calendar + brand guidelines
- Output: published content across platforms
- Deliverable: a running automation that replaces 20–30 hours/week of manual work

Monthly retainer model: you maintain the automation, client provides content direction.
