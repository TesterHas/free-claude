# 5 Ways to Make Money with AI (2026)

**Source:** Sabrina Ramonov — "5 Ways to Make Money in 2026 (with AI)" (May 2026, 27 min)

## The Frame

Sabrina's primary insight: it's not about which tools you use or how technical you are. It's about which of the two paths you pick (see [[9-ai-businesses]] for the Path A / Path B breakdown).

Her own example: ~2 million followers across social media, thousands of paying app users, zero employees. She built this with AI.

## Directional signals (from the video)

The five ways align with the broader pattern she covers in her 9 AI businesses video:

1. Content + audience → paid products (courses, community)
2. AI-powered SaaS/apps
3. AI freelancing / done-for-you services
4. Automation agency (outreach to retainer)
5. AI-enhanced consulting (package your expertise into AI systems)

## Key takeaway for sales

The "ways to make money" framing is actually a client discovery tool. When you're prospecting:
- What is this person already doing?
- Which of these 5 paths do they most naturally fit?
- Which path is closest to their existing skills?

Then scope your AI system to accelerate that specific path — not a generic automation.
