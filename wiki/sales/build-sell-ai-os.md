# Build & Sell AI Operating Systems

**Source:** Nate Herk — "Build & Sell Claude Code Operating Systems (2+ Hour Course)" (May 2026)

## The Product

An "AI Operating System" (AIOS) in Nate's terminology is a configured Claude Code environment — skills, agents, hooks, memory, integrations — that functions as a deployed product you can sell to clients or businesses.

Not just "using Claude." A packaged, repeatable system.

## Why this is the right productization

Nate's background:
- Scaled an AI automation agency to $100K/month, then sold it
- Now runs one of the largest AI communities in the world (350K+ members, free)

His current thesis: the leverage is in the operating system layer, not individual automations. Building skills and agents that encode your best practices means every client gets your full expertise, not just your time.

## What to sell

The AIOS itself is the deliverable. You scope it, build it, hand it over — or maintain it on retainer. The differentiation:
- Industry-specific knowledge baked into CLAUDE.md and skills
- Pre-built agents for that industry's common workflows
- Ongoing "leveling up" as new Claude features drop

## How to price it

Framing: you're not selling AI software, you're selling an AI team member. The ROI conversation is "what does a junior analyst cost vs. what does this system do in the same time?" The answer is usually 10–100x leverage.

## For selling to SMBs

Package three tiers:
1. **Starter** — CLAUDE.md + core skills, your templates, 1 agent. One-time setup fee.
2. **Growth** — Full AIOS, custom agents, 30-day onboarding support.
3. **Managed** — Monthly retainer, you maintain and upgrade as Claude improves.
