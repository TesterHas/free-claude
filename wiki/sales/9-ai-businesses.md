# 9 AI Businesses That Work Right Now

**Source:** Sabrina Ramonov — "These 9 AI Businesses Will Make You $1M (With Zero Employees)" (April 2026, 67 min)

## The Core Frame

Sabrina runs this through a two-path decision:

**Path A** — For people who never want to cold DM a stranger. Build content and inbound. Slower start, compounding.

**Path B** — For people who need income this week. Outreach-based, faster to first dollar.

The choice isn't about which is better — it's about which fits your current situation.

## Real-world benchmarks she cites

- Freelancer: $30,000/month from 7 clients
- Paid AI community: $4,000,000/year
- Vibe-coded app: thousands in monthly recurring revenue
- Sabrina's own app: thousands of paying users, ~2 million social followers, zero employees

## The 9 businesses (framework)

Both paths have variants within them. Path A businesses tend to be:
- AI-powered content creation (social, YouTube, newsletters)
- SaaS/apps built with AI
- Paid communities and courses

Path B businesses tend to be:
- AI automation agency (outreach → retainer)
- Freelance AI development
- Done-for-you AI systems

## Sales angle

The key insight for selling AI systems: the path matters more than the tools. Prospects who keep asking "which AI tool should I use?" are asking the wrong question. The real question is: do they need revenue now (Path B → automation agency, start with one retainer) or do they want equity-style compounding (Path A → build something that scales without them)?

Diagnose the path first, then scope the build.
