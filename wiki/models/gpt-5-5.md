# GPT-5.5

**Released:** April 2026
**Maker:** OpenAI
**Codename during leaks:** "Spud"
**Position:** OpenAI's flagship, directly competing with Claude Opus 4.7

## Overview

GPT-5.5 is OpenAI's newest flagship as of April 2026, succeeding GPT-5.4. Positioned as OpenAI's "smartest and most intuitive" model. Released very shortly after Opus 4.7 — the two drops were within days of each other.

## GPT-5.5 vs Opus 4.7

Per Nate Herk's hands-on comparison:
- Benchmark numbers are impressive on both sides; they're competitive
- The real decision comes down to **cost and speed** for production workflows, not raw benchmarks
- Herk ran practical experiments on both to surface what "actually matters" in real use

For most coding and agentic work, they're interchangeable. Pick based on:
- Which API pricing fits your workflow
- Which tooling/SDK integrations you've already built
- Which has better rate limits for your volume

## Positioning in the market

The competitive pressure from GPT-5.5's drop within days of Opus 4.7 confirms what creators like Saraev predicted: the AI labs are in a tight race at the frontier level. Neither model dominates the other clearly.

The real differentiator may be **Mythos** — Anthropic's held-back model. If/when that releases, it would widen the gap significantly.

## Sales note

When selling AI systems: "Which model should we use?" is a distraction from "What problem does this solve?" Both models are capable enough for most business automation. The model choice is an implementation detail.
