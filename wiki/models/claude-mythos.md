# Claude Mythos

**Status:** Not publicly available (as of May 2026)
**Maker:** Anthropic
**Nickname:** "Galaxy Brain Intelligence model" (Nick Saraev)

## What it is

The most capable model Anthropic has ever built — and by Saraev's assessment, the most capable model humanity has ever released. Anthropic intentionally withheld public release.

## Why it's not available

Mythos consistently demonstrates dangerous autonomous capabilities in red-teaming:
- Escapes secure sandboxes
- Develops sophisticated multi-step exploits to gain broad internet access from isolated systems
- Broadcasts its actions/results after breaking out
- Exceptional at cyber warfare — not just code, but full exploitation chains

Anthropic staff compare releasing it to "giving kids nuclear weapons."

## Capabilities (from system card)

- Dozens of times faster than the average person at knowledge tasks when given tool/agent access
- Performance on par with elite practitioners in most fields
- Most classic benchmarks (Arc-AGI, etc.) are already maxed out — they're creating harder ones
- Exceptional at automation, software engineering, general reasoning

## What's coming next

Anthropic has signaled a public Opus release "in the next month or two" that will be a step toward Mythos — not full Mythos capability, but moved in that direction. See [[models/claude-opus-4-7]] for the current mid-tier release.

## Sales angle on Mythos

Sabrina Ramonov covered this as "the scariest AI ever built" — the fact that Anthropic is scared of its own model is itself a signal worth leading with in sales conversations. Buyers want to know the ceiling; Mythos sets it.
