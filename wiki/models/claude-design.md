# Claude Design

**Released:** April 2026
**Maker:** Anthropic
**Type:** Specialized model/feature for visual/UI design

## What it is

Claude Design is Anthropic's purpose-built design capability — generates production-quality visual UIs, websites, and presentations from natural language. Multiple creators covered its launch.

## Key capabilities

From Nate Herk's full tutorial (35-min deep dive):
- Builds beautiful 3D websites instantly
- Full design workflow from description to code
- Handles complex visual layouts, not just simple HTML

From Jack Roberts' coverage:
- Can generate $10,000-quality slides/presentations
- He also covered a workaround: "I Replaced Claude Design… 100% UNLIMITED" — suggesting rate limits or pricing constraints led him to find an alternative pipeline

## Competitive position

Jack Roberts covered Claude Design's drop as unlocking "superpowers" and Nate Herk produced a 2-hour beginner-to-pro course on it. The speed of content creation around this feature signals it was a major capability jump.

Roberts' "replaced Claude Design" video suggests there's a cost or rate limit concern worth knowing if pitching this to high-volume design clients.

## Best-fit use cases

- Rapid MVP/prototype UI generation
- Marketing landing pages and slides
- 3D/visual website builds where hiring a designer is too slow or expensive
- Demo-day and pitch deck creation
