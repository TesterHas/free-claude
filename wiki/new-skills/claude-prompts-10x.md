## Key prompting patterns (from Sabrina's broader content)

1. **Genius mode activation** — explicit prompting that unlocks deeper reasoning
2. **Role + context front-loading** — giving Claude full context before the task, not after
3. **Constraint setting** — telling Claude what NOT to do as much as what to do
4. **Chain-of-thought elicitation** — explicitly asking for reasoning steps
5. **Iteration framing** — asking for multiple versions/approaches, not just one answer
6. **Self-critique loop** — having Claude review its own output before delivering
7. **Output format specification** — explicit format requirements reduce post-processing

## Why this matters for building AI systems

Prompt engineering is the layer that makes the same model 2–10x more useful. When building client systems:

- Most value is unlocked by well-crafted instructions, not just model choice
